Fiction books are based on imagination rather than fact. They include genres such as mystery, science fiction, fantasy, romance, and historical fiction.These books focus on storytelling,character development, and plot, allowing readers to experience different worlds and ideas.

Nonfiction books present factual information. Examples include biographies, self-help books, history texts, and scientific works. Their goal is to inform,educate, or persuade the reader through real events and data.

Romance novels are another major category, especially in digital publishing. Many readers seek comfort, optimism, and emotional satisfaction, making romance one of the best-selling genres globally.

Educational or academic books are designed for structured learning. Textbooks and scholarly works explain subjects in detail, often including exercises, examples, and citations to support study and teaching.
